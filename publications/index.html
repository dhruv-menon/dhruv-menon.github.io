publications

in science, writing is the ‘only thing we do that matters’. i spend a great deal of time thinking and writing - and as a consequence, i’m quite active in publishing my work. while my articles generally focus on computational aspects of materials discovery & design for drug delivery, gas capture & separation, and catalysis, i also publish reviews & commentaries on broader aspects of biology, energy & environment.

i try to write in ways that are accessible to general audiences (and feel that i’m getting better at it with time). in case you aren’t able to access my papers for whatever reason, drop me an email at dm958[at]cam[dot]ac[dot]uk.
